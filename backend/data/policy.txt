SHOPSPHERE REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

RULE 1: STANDARD REFUND WINDOW

Customers may request a refund only within 30 calendar days of the product delivery date.

Any refund request submitted after the 30-day refund window has expired is not eligible for approval.

Keywords: refund window, refund period, delivery date, late refund request, eligibility.

Outcome:

* Eligible if requested within 30 days of delivery.
* Denied if requested after 30 days.

RULE 2: ORDER VERIFICATION

A valid order ID must be provided for every refund request.

The customer's name or email must match the order information stored in the CRM system.

Refund requests cannot be processed when the order cannot be verified or when customer information does not match the order record.

Keywords: order verification, order ID, customer verification, CRM lookup, identity validation.

Outcome:

* Eligible if order information is verified successfully.
* Denied if order verification fails.

RULE 3: PRODUCT CONDITION

Physical products are eligible for refund when they are returned in acceptable condition.

Products that were intentionally damaged, altered, or misused by the customer are not eligible for a refund.

Products that arrived damaged because of shipping or carrier-related issues remain eligible for a refund.

Keywords: damaged product, shipping damage, physical product, return condition, customer damage.

Outcome:

* Eligible for shipping-related damage.
* Denied for intentional customer damage.

RULE 4: DIGITAL PRODUCTS

Digital products, online courses, downloadable files, software licenses, and digital content become non-refundable after activation, download, access, or usage.

Customers may request a refund only before the digital product has been activated or downloaded.

Keywords: digital product, downloaded content, activated license, online course, software refund.

Outcome:

* Eligible before activation or download.
* Denied after activation, download, or usage.

RULE 5: REFUND LIMITS

To prevent abuse of the refund system, customers may receive a maximum of two approved refunds within any rolling twelve-month period.

Customers who have already received more than two approved refunds during the last twelve months are not eligible for additional refunds.

Keywords: refund limit, refund count, excessive refunds, refund history, customer abuse prevention.

Outcome:

* Eligible if refund count is 2 or fewer.
* Denied if refund count exceeds 2.

RULE 6: HIGH-VALUE ORDERS

Refund requests involving orders with a total purchase value greater than $500 require additional verification and human oversight.

These requests cannot be automatically approved or denied by the support agent and must be escalated for manual review.

Keywords: high value order, expensive purchase, order value above 500 dollars, large transaction, manual review.

Outcome:

* Manual Review for orders greater than $500.

RULE 7: FINAL SALE ITEMS

Products sold as clearance items, liquidation products, final-sale merchandise, or non-returnable inventory are not eligible for refunds.

Customers are informed about final-sale restrictions at the time of purchase.

Keywords: final sale, clearance item, liquidation product, non-returnable item, sale merchandise.

Outcome:

* Denied.

RULE 8: SUBSCRIPTION PRODUCTS

Subscription renewal charges may be refunded only within seven calendar days of the renewal date.

After seven days have passed from the subscription renewal date, refund requests for subscription renewals are not eligible.

Keywords: subscription refund, renewal charge, recurring billing, membership renewal, seven-day window.

Outcome:

* Eligible within 7 days of renewal.
* Denied after 7 days.

RULE 9: FRAUD PREVENTION

Accounts flagged for suspicious activity, fraud indicators, chargeback abuse, identity concerns, or unusual purchasing behavior require additional investigation.

These refund requests cannot be automatically processed and must be escalated for manual review.

Keywords: fraud flag, suspicious account, chargeback abuse, risk review, account investigation.

Outcome:

* Manual Review.

RULE 10: SHIPPING DELAYS

Customers may request a refund when an order is delivered more than fourteen days later than the originally promised delivery date.

The delay must not be caused by incorrect customer-provided delivery information.

Keywords: shipping delay, delayed delivery, late shipment, promised delivery date, carrier delay.

Outcome:

* Eligible when delivery delay exceeds 14 days.

RULE 11: DUPLICATE REFUND REQUESTS

A customer cannot receive multiple refunds for the same order.

If a refund has already been approved, processed, completed, or issued for an order, any additional refund request for that order must be denied.

Keywords: duplicate refund, already refunded, previous refund, multiple refund request, refund processed.

Outcome:

* Denied.

RULE 12: LOST SHIPMENTS

Customers are eligible for a refund when the shipping carrier officially confirms that a package has been lost in transit and cannot be delivered.

Carrier confirmation is required before processing the refund.

Keywords: lost shipment, lost package, carrier confirmation, package missing, shipment lost.

Outcome:

* Eligible.

RULE 13: EXCHANGE COMPLETED

Orders that have already received a completed product exchange are not eligible for an additional refund.

Customers must choose either an exchange or a refund, but not both.

Keywords: exchange completed, exchanged item, replacement product, refund after exchange.

Outcome:

* Denied.

RULE 14: GIFT PURCHASES

Refund requests for gift purchases may only be submitted by the original purchaser.

Gift recipients, gift receivers, or third parties are not authorized to request refunds on behalf of the purchaser.

Keywords: gift purchase, gift recipient, original purchaser, gift refund request.

Outcome:

* Eligible only for original purchaser.
* Denied for gift recipient.

RULE 15: MANUAL REVIEW CONDITIONS

The refund support system must escalate requests for manual review whenever policy conditions are conflicting, unclear, or require human judgment.

Manual review is required when:

* Order value exceeds $500.
* Fraud indicators or suspicious account activity exist.
* Multiple policy rules conflict.
* The customer requests a policy exception.
* The agent cannot confidently determine eligibility.

Keywords: manual review, policy exception, conflicting rules, escalation, human approval.

Outcome:

* Manual Review.
